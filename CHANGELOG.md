## [0.1.0] - 2020-09-12
### Added
- Added 3 unique challenges in the form of "cursed" perks
    - Heartbreak: maximum health cannot be increased
    - Bane of Midas: gold is deadly and must be avoided
    - Mortality: take damage to maximum health
